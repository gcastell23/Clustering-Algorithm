IGMC

Information-Geometric Multiscale Clustering

🔬 CMU Pre-College Comp Bio | Team: Gabriella, Megan, Emma, Rajarshi

🚀 Project Pillars

1. The Smart Ruler 📏

Replaces straight-line grid systems with a dynamic geometric metric. Bends around noise to amplify rare biological signals.

2. The Ink-Drop Test 💧

Employs random walks to find stable biological communities, eliminating arbitrary clustering knobs.

3. Islands vs. Highways 🛣️

Finds whether clusters represent isolated cell classes (islands) or transitioning pathways (highways).

4. Calibrated Forecasts 🌤️

Outputs highly calibrated statistical ranges giving clear confidence maps over predictions.
